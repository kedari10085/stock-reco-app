Cancellation & Refunds - Lift

Last Updated: October 23, 2025

Free Plan Cancellation

Free plan users can cancel their account at any time without any charges. Simply contact our support team or use the account deletion option in your profile.

Premium Plan Cancellation

Important: Premium plans for early users (first 1000) are FREE forever and cannot be cancelled once activated.

For users who paid ₹299/month, you can cancel your subscription at any time. The cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.

Personal Assistant Plan Cancellation

Personal Assistant subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. The cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period. You will continue to have access to the service until the end of the paid period.

Refund Policy

No Refunds: Due to the nature of our digital services, we do not offer refunds for subscription fees once the service has been accessed.

Exceptional Cases

In rare exceptional circumstances (technical issues preventing service access for more than 48 hours), refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Early User Protection: First 1000 users receive FREE Premium access forever with no cancellation or refund concerns.

How to Cancel

  1. Contact our support team at liftsuppor@gmail.com or +91-9291260066
  2. Provide your account email and reason for cancellation
  3. Our team will process your cancellation within 24 hours
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once cancelled

Account Deletion

If you wish to completely delete your account and all associated data, please email us with "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line. This action cannot be undone.
